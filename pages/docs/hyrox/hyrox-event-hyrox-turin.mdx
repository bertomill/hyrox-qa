# HYROX Turin | HYROX

![](https://hyrox.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/03/HYROX-Logo.svg)
Event Page
![](https://maintain.hyrox.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/HYROX_Wordmark_TURIN_White-Transp.png)

30\. Jan. 2026– 1\. Feb. 2026

Turin, we’re back, bigger,  better and  bolder!

For the third time, HYROX is returning to the iconic Oval Lingotto, bringing its electrifying fitness race back to one of Italy’s most impressive venues. At the end of January, prepare to push your limits in this unique competition that blends strength, endurance, and functional athleticism.

HYROX challenges athletes to complete eight functional workouts, each uniquely designed, and all preceded by a 1km run. It’s a true test of overall fitness that demands both grit and strategy. Whether you’re a seasoned HYROX veteran aiming for a new personal best or a newcomer ready to take on the ultimate challenge, the Oval Lingotto’s expansive and inspiring setting provides the perfect backdrop for an unforgettable experience.

Don’t miss your chance to be part of the HYROX family and witness the incredible dedication and power of athletes from all over. The atmosphere will be electric, the competition fierce, and the sense of accomplishment immense.

Get ready to run, push, pull, and jump your way to the finish line in Turin!
