### The Fitness Person

You’re in the gym more days than not—lifting, running, sweating, pushing. Fitness isn’t just something you do; it’s who you are. But all those reps and miles are starting to feel repetitive. You’re searching for something bigger, a challenge that turns your discipline into something greater.

This is it. The Sport You Are Already Training For.

My Sport is HYROX.

[Find My Race](https://hyrox.com/find-my-race/)

**The gym is a non-negotiable for you. Whether it’s seven days a week or three, fitness is your anchor. You thrive on setting goals—faster times, heavier lifts, harder workouts. It’s not just about feeling strong; it’s about proving to yourself what you can achieve.**

**But lately, you’ve been craving more. All the hard work needs a purpose, a finish line. What if you could take everything you’ve built—every class, every mile, every rep—and channel it into something extraordinary?**

**HYROX is The Sport You Are Already Training For: a global fitness competition designed for athletes like you. It’s a test of endurance, strength, and grit that gives new meaning to your routine and connects you to a community of like-minded challengers.**

**Training isn’t just part of your life. It’s your lifestyle. Now, it’s time to put it to the ultimate test.**

**Your Sport is HYROX.**
