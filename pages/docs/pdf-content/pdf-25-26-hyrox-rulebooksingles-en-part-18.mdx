11 CLOTHING,
ACCESSORIES,

It is the responsibility of the racer to ensure that
they are appropriately dressed when preparing for
the activities to be undertaken, in relation to the
environmental factors and any cultural sensitivities
that should be observed.
All racers are required to wear closed-toe shoes at all
times during the race, except at the Wall Ball workout
station where racers may remove their shoes to
complete the Wall Balls.
