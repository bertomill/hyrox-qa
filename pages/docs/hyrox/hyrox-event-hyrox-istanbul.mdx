ISTANBUL’a geliyoruz!

The World’s Fitness Race is Coming to One of the World’s Greatest Cities.

HYROX is finally arriving in ISTANBUL, a city of power, passion, and purpose.

A place where continents meet, history lives on, and energy fills the air. And now, for the very first time, the global HYROX community will race together in Türkiye.

This is your call to be part of history in the making.

Whether you are chasing your personal best, racing with your HYROX Doubles partner, or stepping onto the floor for the very first time, Istanbul will deliver an experience like no other. Get ready for loud cheers, high energy, and the unique atmosphere of a city that never stops

moving.

Race in a legendary city connecting Europe and Asia. Experience top-level competition. Push beyond your limits.

Join thousands of athletes from around the world in a city where old meets new and show what you are made of on the HYROX stage. This is not just another race. This is ISTANBUL.

Are you in?
