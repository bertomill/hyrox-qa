**Get Ready for HYROX Bengaluru!**

After a thrilling debut in May 2025 and an electrifying race in Delhi this July, HYROX makes its way to India’s _Garden City_ to close out the 2025–26 season on **April 11, 2026**.

Whether you’re chasing a new personal best or stepping into your first HYROX arena, Bengaluru is your stage. Go solo in the HYROX Open or Pro divisions, partner up for HYROX Doubles, or bring the energy with your squad in the HYROX Relay. There’s a race for everybody.

Registrations are opening soon. Are you ready to step up, show up, and take on the world’s ultimate fitness race?

Your Race Package & On-Site Experience

- The coveted HYROX finishers patch
- Your result time posted on the leaderboard as you compete
- Individual finisher time with split timings for each workout
- Age Group award ceremony in each division
- Qualification slots for certain Age Groups in the Pro divisions for the World Championships
- Aid stations throughout the course: pre, during and post-event
- Enjoy a spectacular view from the Thunderdome to watch the other racers compete throughout the day
- HYROX Entertainment Zone with our partners
- Opportunities to purchase professional photos of your hard work

And much more!
