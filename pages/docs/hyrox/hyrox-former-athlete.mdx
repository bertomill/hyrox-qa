### The former Athlete

You’re an athlete—always have been, always will be. Whether you’re on the field, the track, or the gym floor, competition is in your blood. HYROX isn’t here to replace your sport—it’s here to amplify it. Take your skills to a new arena and discover how far your drive can take you.

Your Sport is HYROX.

We are the athletes who never stop competing. From the court to the pool to the pavement, we’ve chased goals and crushed limits. HYROX is our next chapter—a challenge that unites us, pushes us, and keeps the fire alive.

Our Sport is HYROX.

I’ve been competing for as long as I can remember. My sport gave me focus, shaped my identity, and taught me what it means to strive. Now, I’m looking for the next challenge—a new way to channel my passion and grow. HYROX is where my competitive spirit thrives.

My Sport is HYROX.

**Being an athlete isn’t something you grow out of—it’s part of who you are. Whether you’re still racing, scoring, or competing, or if you’ve stepped away from your sport, the fire to challenge yourself never goes out. But sometimes, you hit a plateau or feel the need for something new. That’s where HYROX comes in.**

**HYROX isn’t here to replace your sport—it’s here to complement it. It’s holistic fitness with a competitive edge. It’s a chance to apply everything you’ve built—strength, speed, discipline—to a new kind of race.**

**Every event is a stage. Every finish line is a test. And every athlete shares your hunger for more.**

**At HYROX, you’re part of a global community of competitors who push each other to new limits. Whether you’re training for personal bests or seeking camaraderie among like-minded athletes, HYROX is your next arena.**
