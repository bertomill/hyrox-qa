# HYROX TOULOUSE | HYROX

![](https://hyrox.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/03/HYROX-Logo.svg)
Event Page
19\. Mar. 2026– 22\. Mar. 2026

**Get ready, HYROX is coming back to Toulouse – stronger, louder, and more electrifying than ever!**

We’re excited to announce the **2nd edition of HYROX Toulouse**, taking place from **March 19th to 22nd** – four action-packed days where fitness meets competition in the pink city !

After a successful debut, HYROX is returning to bring the heat in the South of France. Whether you’re a seasoned athlete or a first-time competitor, this is your chance to take on the HYROX challenge – solo, in doubles, or in relay. Test your strength, speed, and mental grit in an event like no other.

**New year, new goals. Same HYROX intensity.**

The Toulouse arena is calling – are you ready to rise to the occasion?

Ticket sales are opening soon, so start building your team or prepare to go it alone. Singles Open, Pro, Doubles, Relay – whatever your format, your HYROX journey starts now.

**Join the movement. Make your mark. See you in Toulouse !**
