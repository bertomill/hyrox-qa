## BECOME A HYROX TRAINING CLUB

**H** **YROX TRAINING CLUBS** are the backbone of the global HYROX community.

Accredited HYROX TRAINING CLUBS are affiliated facilities that can advertise and market their services whilst leveraging the HYROX brand name.

All official HYROX TRAINING CLUBS are granted access to the HYROX Performance Hub where they have access to an evergreen library of daily programming tools, class tutorials, marketing materials and more…

These are tools for gym owners and coaches to implement HYROX-CENTERic training and upskill themselves to become performance leaders in the sport of fitness racing.
